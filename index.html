About

A developer who ships

I'm an iOS developer based in Egypt, building for startups in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. For four and a half years I've taken products from a founder's first idea to a running business — scoping the work, shaping the feature set, then owning the build the whole way: architecture, Swift & SwiftUI, API integration, payments, and release.

The part most people skip: I stay. Launch is the start, not the finish. Engagements typically run one to two years, several clients are still with me today, and the product keeps shipping on a continuous release cycle long after version 1.0.

Weekly reviews with founders and their marketing and operations teams keep the product tracking the business, not just the backlog. And I instrument what I build — Firebase Analytics and A/B testing — so marketing can watch real funnels, test a change against real users, and decide from data instead of opinion.

I care about clean architecture, interfaces that feel effortless, and the discipline of getting things live. Whether it's a single focused app or a multi vendor marketplace, the goal is the same: ship something people trust.
